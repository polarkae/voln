      World Famous Jaipuri Pure Cotton Double Bed Razai (Quilt) In Traditional Sanganeri Print Of Rajasthan. This Spectacular Quilt Will Enhance The Beauty Of Your Home Decor As Well As Provide You Warmth On Cooler Winter Nights. The Lightweight Jaipuri Quilt Is Filled With Pure 100% Cotton & Outer Fabric Has Been Used Of Pure Cotton.It provides a great deal of warmth even in the very cold weather primarily due to the insulating effects of the large amount of air trapped in the cotton. These good looking, fluffy, hand-made Indian quilts are a speciality of Jaipur made by traditional craftsmen with perfection which can easily be felt while using the product. Stitching design on the razai has been done with the perfection so that the trapped cotton doesnt lose its place which makes the razai more durable and adds more life to the product. The cotton used is feather light which adds beauty to the product which results in requiring less space to store the quilt and is easier to maintain as compared to traditional old fashioned heavy weight quilts. This mesmerising product can be used throughout the year even in the AC while sleeping. The cotton used as filling is world class which is longer lasting also it doesnt lose its shape even if the quilt is used for a very very long time.. This Quilt Is A Perfect Gift For Weddings, Housewarmings, Engagements And Anniversaries
